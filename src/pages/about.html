About

I believe that therapy offers a sacred space for one to be heard and wholly accepted. I view my role as empowering you to make changes in your life and honoring the path of your healing.

My style of therapy is to fully listen and to provide a reflection of what I observe and gently challenge patterns of behaviors that keep you from living a life that is fulfilling. I meet you where you are at and give you ownership to set the pace of your work.

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree from the University of California Davis and a Masters in Marriage and Family Therapy from Western Seminary. In the last four years, I have had the privilege to work alongside children, teens, and families in Fremont, Santa Clara County, and in Campbell - providing counseling in various settings such as outpatient clinic, elementary and high schools.
